About This Application

What is AQI?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a standardized indicator of air quality in a given area. It tells you how clean or polluted the air is, and what associated health effects might be of concern.

AQI Categories

AQI RangeCategoryHealth Impact
0-50GoodMinimal impact
51-100ModerateAcceptable for most
101-150Unhealthy for Sensitive GroupsVulnerable people affected
151-200UnhealthyHealth effects for everyone
201-300Very UnhealthyEmergency conditions
301-500HazardousSerious health effects
